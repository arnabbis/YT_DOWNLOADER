Frequently asked questions

Is this safe to use?

Yes. The backend validates input, strips unsafe characters, and keeps downloads isolated in temporary folders.

Can I trim before download?

Absolutely. Both video and audio downloads can be clipped using FFmpeg-based trimming controls.

What kinds of links are supported?

YouTube links from youtube.com and youtu.be are validated before analysis begins.

Can I download any YouTube video?

Only download videos you own or are authorized to save, and follow YouTube's terms and local copyright rules.
